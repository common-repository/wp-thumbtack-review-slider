Easily create a slider of your Thumbtack business reviews to show in your Posts, Pages, and Widget areas!

== Description ==

This plugin aims to do one thing as simply and easily as possible. It uses your existing Thumbtack business reviews and creates a super cool testimonial slider or grid out of them. After all, who really trusts testimonials unless they know where they are coming from.

Thumbtack allows you to find local professionals in your area for just about any kind of job or service. It also allows customers to rate the local business or professional, which is where this plugin comes in.

Why not display your Thumbtack reviews on your WordPress site? You work hard for them after all!

Some of the Features:

* Display the Thumbtack reviews in a slider or simple grid.
* Download 5 Thumbtack reviews and display in a Post or Page with a shortcode or template code.
* Plugin will automatically check for new reviews every night and add them to your database.
* Create an unlimited number of review templates that you can add in different locations across your site.
* Each template is easily customizable and you can even add custom CSS if you want.
* Template display options like:  border radius, show/hide date, show/hide star rating, background and text colors.
* Show mulitple Thumbtack reviews per a row and even have mulitiple rows.
* Only show reviews with text.
* Sort the reviews randomly or by newest.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `wp-thumbtack-reviews` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Look for the WP Thumbtack Reviews menu item in your admin.
